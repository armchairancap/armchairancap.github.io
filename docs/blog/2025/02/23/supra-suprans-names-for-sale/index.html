Buy my SupraNS names

February 23, 2025 · 3 min read
ArmchairAncap

I've registered a few SupraNS names to keep them until those who want them decide to buy them... or not.

info

SupraNS is the officicial name system of the Supra network.

Its function is similar to what ENS does on the Ethereum network: initially it will be used for wallet address aliasing.

You can find more about it at the link below and in the official documentation (linked from the site below).

Currently Supra has just one wallet, StarKey, and StarKey is about to add SupraNS support for addresses in coming days or weeks.

At that time SupraNS names will become useful. Currently they have just one use: speculation, which is what I've been doing.

Another important feature that's still missing is the ability to transfer SupraNS registration ownership.

Because of that there are no marketplaces for SupraNS trading either, but that's precisely why I listed mine on a Web site: I've no idea when that may become available which is why I'm posting those SupraNS names on a Web site.

See the list of my SupraNS names at https://SupraNS.name. Screenshot:

By now you may beginning to wonder how can one sell a SupraNS name when transfer isn't possible. I plan to use escrow service provided by someone in the Supra community, and will commit to transferring the name if the agreed-upon payment is promptly made to the person who helps with escrow.

Transfers can't happen before the API becomes available, but prices may be agreed upon and funds transferred before SupraNS becomes popular and before Internet auctions become available.

info

The site has the prices, too, but those are not fixed (as explained on the site).

I'll probably use them as initial prices in auctions on a SupraNS marketplace.

To be honest, I don't think the prices of SupraNS names will be anywhere close to Solana's (let alone Ethereum's), but that's not relevant.

Initially SupraNS registrations were very cheap. After the first 10,000 registrations were reached the doubled the prices and they'll hike them again after 15,000 registrations which should happen within days of StarKey adding support for SupraNS. Now most StarKey users have no idea about SupraNS.

I would definiively recommend SupraNS to StarKey users and to anyone who runs a business on Supra because it's a useful feature. It's been useful on all chains and it will be on this one as well. But speculating in hem may be risky.

For that same reason I'm not going to hype these or claim that these "may" become a source of huge profits (10-baggers!). I don't think that will happen. Buy 'em if you need 'em.

note

I actually have more than those that I've listed at SupraNS.name, but for various reasons I'm not rushing to sell them.

More on that and other SupraNS topics in a future blog post when SupraNS becomes trade-able.
