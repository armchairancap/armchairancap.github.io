Supra's SupraNova bridgeless cross-chain dApp is live

August 12, 2025 · 4 min read
ArmchairAncap

After weeks of delays, it's finally here.

Go to https://supranova.ai.

Connect Metamask, have some ETH for gas/fees and at least some amount of one of the supported coins/tokens. I chose ERC20 USDC - had about $2 in that wallet. My destination currency was supUSDC, on Supra L1, of course. Connect StarKey as well (both on Mainnet).

Remember this takes "two taps" - the usual Metamask thing where the second transaction is the actual transaction.

note

The first "overhead" transaction on Ethereum L1 cost me $0.54, while the other "real" transaction cost $2.30. Ethereum is garbage.

Metamask will offer to open Ethernet explorer for each transaction, which you can reject. But you may as well do it since you have nothing else to do until it's done. You need to wait a couple of minutes.

SupraNova's estimate was 4 minutes, but it took around 6-7.

That's on the slow side (see the recent stats here) - most SupraNova transactions take less time. Mine took over 200 seconds while the current average for transactions from last 60 minutes is 136 seconds. But this is Day 1 and all other chains are slower.

Next problem: where's my supUSDC, sir?

At first I thought I should get token contract info for USDC from Etherscan.io and add the sucker (0xA0b86991c6218b36c1d19D4a2e9Eb0cE3606eB48) to StarKey. Wait - of course not! We're supposed to see this on the Supra blockchain, not on Ethereum!

My StarKey is up to date, so it's not that. It was time to get to work...

I found the crediting transaction (Destination TX hash in SupraNova.ai transaction details) on SupraScan.io, opened it, and looked in the Events (rather than Advanced Information) tab. In Index 4, I found it!

{
    data:{
    amount:"",
    minter_addr:"0xc063898f46a022d136feaea15d8c311eafaf15447dec0ef26b43d54b39fd821a",
    wrapped_token_addr:"0xf90b4b9d4a9d87c39fb3140513e52edc3ead5eaddcb9881b02becdeb63c5793d"
    },
    guid:{
    account_address:"0x0",
    creation_number:"0"
    },
    type:"0xda20f7d0ec813c751926f06004a10bc6ee1eefc96798f6a1aa31447ee146f932::wrapped_token_deployer::WrappedTokenMintedEvent",
    sequence_number:"0"
}

Yesss!

I added a Supra token with the contract 0xf90b4b9d4a9d87c39fb3140513e52edc3ead5eaddcb9881b02becdeb63c5793d to Starkey and...

Very nice!

The only problem I had using SupraNova was my StarKey was flipping to Testnet from time to time. That's probably because I used to access SupraNova.ai on Testnet. I've since deleted and then connected again, but haven't done new transactions to see if that helped. If you haven't used Testnet SupraNova, you're unlikely to be affected by this.

What now?​

Well, a bunch of good things.

You can pay someone with supUSDC without paying Ethereum's scam-like gas fees, you can robo-trade Supra<->supUSDC with AutoFi with constantly low transaction fees, and so on...

One major thing that can't be done now - but is a roadmap item to be delivered within weeks - is the ability to go back, supUSDC->USDC (ERC20) (and the like for other supported currencies and tokens). One minor thing is that on Day 1 only several (major) tokens are supported, basically ETH and USDC. That's enough for now, but I'd like to be able to see other ERC20 tokens supported soon (I'd still like to see WXX added, for example, and fund its LP on a Supra DEX such as Dexlyn or Atmos).

Supra is in a good place now. Even though EVM hasn't hit Mainnet yet, AutoFi and SupraNova are extremely powerful services.

I am not into day trading and don't like to discuss the price here, but I noticed some people have been "selling the news" and "after all, BTC has dropped as well". That is ridiculous! Given its valuation and these services, Supra is dramatically undervalued in relative terms (meaning, maybe all crypto is worthless, but relatively speaking, some seem under-valued). Even if Bitcoin and Ethereum crashed 50% Supra still ought to appreciate several times over and it would still be relatively under-valued. Let's watch TVL and see what happens in coming weeks.
