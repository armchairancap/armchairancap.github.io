What are xx Network Channels? Visit xx Network Speakeasy User's Guide to find out!

Below you can find some Speakeasy channels known to me. Note the following:

  • All channels listed are public unless noted otherwise
  • These channels are not controlled, managed, or moderated by ArmchairAncap unless noted otherwise
  • I don't necessarily use these channels myself. I can only recommend the official channels.
caution

It is impossible to know in advance what may get posted in each channel. Each channel is visited before it's shared, but we can't check them daily and remove them the moment someone posts copyrighted or other illegal content before one of the admins reacts. People post junk, admin keys leak or get stolen, channels get abandoned by their administrators.

Exercise prudence and caution when visiting channels you don't know anything about!

Some low-volume channels may be temporarily empty because messages expire after 21 days.

Official xx Network channels​

  • xx Network General Chat - public chat channel managed by the xx Foundation. Actively managed and moderated by Foundation members. Use it to ask xx Network- or Speakeasy-related questions or say hi.

ArmchairAncap's channels​

  • AACC - this is ArmchairAncap's Chat Channel, if you need to reach out. The official xx Network General Chat (link above) is the best place to ask xx Network-related questions, but if I have time I will also answer your questions about xx Network.
  • AARPS - ArmchairAncap's Reward Payout Service (for validators and nominators on xx Network). If you want to automate daily validator rewards payout for validator wallet you care about, reach out! Payouts are made daily, minutes after an era ends. Fees:
    • 30xx per validator wallet/address per quarter
    • 100xx per validator wallet/address per year (12 months)
    • Also accepted are top 5 cryptocurrencies from Coin Market Cap and Monero
  • xx Network Validator WoS - that's right, WOS stands for "Wall Of Shame"! Chat about crooked validators and stupid nominators. Open to anyone!
