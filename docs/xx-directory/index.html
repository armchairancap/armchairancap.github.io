xx Network Channel Directory​

info

What are xx Network Spaces (or "channels")? Visit xx Network Haven User's Guide to find out!

If you're looking for a list of xx Network-related resources, please see this list curated by PatCrypt.com.

Below you can find some Haven channels known to me. Note the following:

  • All channels listed are public unless noted otherwise
  • These channels are not controlled, managed, or moderated by ArmchairAncap unless noted otherwise
  • I don't necessarily use these channels myself. I can only recommend the official channel xxGeneralChat because I know it's managed by the xx Foundation.
caution

It is impossible to know in advance what may get posted in each channel. As of late 2024, Haven does not support photos or videos so it's unlikely that you'd see something visually offensive.

Each channel is visited by me before I share it, but I can't check them daily and remove them the moment someone posts copyrighted or other illegal content. Exercise prudence and caution when visiting channels you don't know anything about.

Some low-volume channels may be temporarily empty because messages expire after 21 days.

Why people do not have their Haven space listed​

Haven users don't necessarily advertise their Spaces even if they're not password-protected.

It's practically impossible to "guess" the invite link for a public Space for which you have no invite for.
